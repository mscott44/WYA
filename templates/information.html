Information

Calendars must be public in order to view them on WYA. If you wish to see your own calendar on this website you need to sign into WYA with your gmail account with your main calendar set to public.

To make your calendar public:

    Go to this link
    Click on the three dots next to your first calendar under "My Calendars"
    Go to "Settings and sharing"
    Check the box labeled "Make available to the public" in the "Access Permissions" box
